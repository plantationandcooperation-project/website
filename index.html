Tree Plantation & Why It Is Necessary

Tree plantation plays a vital role in fighting climate change and pollution. Trees absorb carbon dioxide, release oxygen, and improve air quality. They help maintain biodiversity, protect wildlife, prevent soil erosion, and reduce the impact of floods and extreme weather.

To promote tree plantation, we should plant native trees, protect existing forests, participate in plantation drives, and encourage others to do the same. A greener environment ensures a healthier and safer future for all living beings.
